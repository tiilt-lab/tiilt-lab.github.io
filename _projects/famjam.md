      **FamJam!** engages children and their families in a making, learning,
      and bonding experience. Over three sessions, families use low- and
      high-tech tools to create a game based on a family narrative or story.
      We seek to understand how we can meaningfully engage families in
      "making" activities both in facilitated makerspaces and at home.
