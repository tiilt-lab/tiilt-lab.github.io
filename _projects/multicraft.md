    body: >-
      **Multicraft + Tangicraft** works on multimodal interfaces to empower
      individuals with physical impairments to play and create in the popular
      video game Minecraft. Multicraft utilizes Gaze and Speech to replace the
      traditional mouse and keyboard interface. By looking at a location on
      the screen and uttering a command, users can build in the Minecraft
      world. Tangicraft utilizes tangible blocks with webcam detectable codes.
      Users can construct a physical structure in front of a webcam and call
      it in the Minecraft world by pressing a button on an Arduino
      microcontroller. Our team ran user tests to drive
      iteration on our designs.
