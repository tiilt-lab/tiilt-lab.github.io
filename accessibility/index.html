Accessibility

Inclusion is the center of the TIILT Lab's research mission, and we hold this website to the same standard. We want everyone to be able to read about our work, meet our team, and use our materials, regardless of ability or technology.

Our target

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA. Pages are built with semantic HTML, a logical heading structure, text alternatives for images, keyboard-operable navigation, and support for reduced-motion preferences.

Known limitations

  1. Some embedded third-party content (videos, document previews) may not meet the same standard.
  2. Our Scratch-based programming environment is a third-party application with limited screen-reader support.
  3. Older PDF papers in our publications archive may not be fully tagged for assistive technology.

Tell us about a barrier

If anything on this site is difficult for you to use, we genuinely want to know. Email us at tiiltlab@gmail.com and we will do our best to fix the issue or provide the content in a format that works for you.
